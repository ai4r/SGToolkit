Help
  • Input speech text or select a sample text and then click 'Generate' button to synthesize gestures.
  • Add motion or style controls and click 'Apply Control' button to update the gestures.
  • When add motion controls, you can use the motion library where pre-recorded unit gestures are available. D or Delete key to delete the selected track.
  • Click 'Apply rules' to add motion controls automatically accoring to word-motion rules. You may add your own rules.
If you any questions or suggestions, please contact youngwoo@etri.re.kr.
